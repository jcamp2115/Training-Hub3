Training Tips

  • Take your time: EMR systems can be complex. Don't rush through the training modules.
  • Practice regularly: The more you use the system, the more comfortable you'll become.
  • Ask questions: Don't hesitate to reach out to your supervisor or IT support for help.
  • Keep notes: Write down important shortcuts and procedures for quick reference.
  • Stay updated: EMR systems receive regular updates, so keep learning about new features.
